Privacy Policy

Neon Drift

Effective Date: December 15, 2025

Developer: Sourabh Suneja

Summary: Neon Drift respects your privacy. The game does not collect personal data, does not require any device permissions, and only uses anonymous analytics to improve gameplay experience.

1. About the Game

Neon Drift is an arcade-style space game where the player guides a glowing neon particle through the cosmos, aiming to reach the highest possible distance while avoiding dangerous hexagonal obstacles and collecting power-ups along the way.

2. Information Collection and Use

2.1 Personal Information

Neon Drift does not collect, store, or process any personally identifiable information (PII). No account creation, login, or personal details are required to play the game.

2.2 Device Permissions

The game does not request access to any sensitive device features, including:

  • Camera
  • Microphone
  • Location
  • Contacts
  • Media files or storage
  • Phone state or identifiers

2.3 Analytics Data

Neon Drift uses Google Analytics to collect anonymous usage data such as:

  • Device type and OS version
  • Session duration and gameplay interactions
  • Approximate country or region
  • Anonymous identifiers generated by Google

This data cannot be used to identify individual users.

3. Use of Collected Data

  • Improve game balance and difficulty
  • Fix bugs and performance issues
  • Enhance visual and gameplay experience
  • Plan future updates

4. Data Sharing

No personal data is shared with third parties. Analytics data is handled solely by Google Analytics under Google’s privacy policy.

5. Data Security

Since Neon Drift does not store personal data, there is no risk of personal data leakage. All analytics data is securely processed by Google.

6. Children’s Privacy

Neon Drift is suitable for all age groups and does not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Third-Party Services

Google Analytics is the only third-party service used:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

This Privacy Policy may be updated when required. Any changes will be reflected with a revised effective date.
